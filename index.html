About HexForm3D

A finished-print studio for practical, custom, and collectible ideas.

HexForm3D is a 3D printing design and production studio. The business produces original products, personalized items, models from licensed designers, and custom project work ranging from modifications to replacement components.

Project acceptance depends on design files, intended use, material suitability, sizing, printability, and safety. HexForm3D does not produce weapon components or firearm-related parts.
