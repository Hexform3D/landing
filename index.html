About HexForm3D

A 3D printing design and production studio.

HexForm3D produces original products and offers personalized products and models from a curated selection of designers. Custom services can include ground-up modeling, modifying existing designs, recreating discontinued components, business signs, QR/NFC “tap-and-go” signs, functional multi-part assemblies, and cosplay projects.

The current site describes David Gibson as the one-man show behind HexForm3D — a nerdy tinkerer who found 3D printers and taught himself CAD to bring more ideas to life.

Safety/disclaimer note: HexForm3D does not design or manufacture weapons or weapon components. Cosplay items, toys, and props are reviewed separately from functional weapons.
